CloudNova Systems, Inc. is a US-based software company focused on building simple, scalable, and intelligent customer relationship management (CRM) solutions for small and mid-sized businesses.

Founded in 2022 and headquartered in Austin, Texas, CloudNova was created to solve a common problem: modern CRMs are either too complex or too expensive for growing businesses.

Our mission is to help businesses manage leads, customers, and sales pipelines efficiently without requiring technical expertise.

CloudNova CRM is designed for teams with 5–500 employees across industries such as professional services, SaaS, real estate, healthcare services, and e-commerce.

Core values:
- Simplicity over complexity
- Automation where it matters
- Security by default
- Customer-first product design
