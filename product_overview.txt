CloudNova CRM is a cloud-based customer relationship management platform that centralizes customer data, automates sales workflows, and provides actionable insights through AI-powered analytics.

Key Features:
- Lead & Contact Management
- Sales Pipeline Tracking
- Automated Follow-ups
- Email & Calendar Integration
- Team Collaboration Tools
- AI Sales Insights
- Custom Dashboards & Reports

CloudNova is web-based and accessible through modern browsers. A mobile-responsive interface allows usage on tablets and smartphones.

Primary Users:
- Sales teams
- Account managers
- Customer success teams
- Small business owners

CloudNova is optimized for fast onboarding, typically taking less than 30 minutes for new teams.
